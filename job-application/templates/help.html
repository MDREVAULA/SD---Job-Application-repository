Help Center

Find answers to frequently asked questions

  • How do I login to my account? Account

    Click the login button in the top right corner, then enter your email address and password. If you've forgotten your password, click the "Forgot Password" link to reset it.

  • How do I create a new account/signup? Account

    Click the signup button on the homepage and fill in the required information including your name, email address, and password. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

  • I forgot my password. What should I do? Account

    Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address and we'll send you instructions to reset your password. Check your spam folder if you don't see the email within a few minutes.

  • How do I update my profile information? Settings

    Navigate to Settings from your account dashboard. From there, you can update your name, email, profile picture, and other personal information. Don't forget to save your changes!

  • How do I change my email preferences? Settings

    Go to Settings > Notifications to customize which emails you receive. You can choose to receive updates about new features, promotions, or turn off all marketing emails while keeping essential account notifications.

  • What payment methods do you accept? Billing

    We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, and PayPal. All transactions are encrypted and secure.

  • How do I cancel my subscription? Billing

    Go to Settings > Billing and click "Cancel Subscription". You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period. We're sorry to see you go!

  • Where can I find tutorials and guides? Resources

    Visit our Learning Center from the main navigation menu. We have video tutorials, step-by-step guides, and best practices to help you get the most out of our platform.
